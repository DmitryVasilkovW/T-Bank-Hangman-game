elephant - the largest land animal with a trunk
crocodile - a large reptile that lives in rivers, has sharp teeth
kangaroo - a marsupial animal that hops and has a pouch
hippopotamus - a large, heavy animal that lives in water, has a big mouth
rhinoceros - a large animal with thick skin and one or two horns
chimpanzee - a highly intelligent primate, closely related to humans
alligator - a reptile similar to a crocodile, but with a broader snout
porcupine - an animal with sharp quills for defense
armadillo - a small mammal with a hard shell, rolls into a ball
orangutan - a large, orange-colored primate that lives in trees
